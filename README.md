### Privacy Policy

_Last updated: July 2026_

### 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the data of individuals ("User", "you") who use the Curtain application (the "App") is handled in accordance with Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK") and related legislation. Curtain is a tool that detects and masks personal data in documents directly on your device. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Policy.

### 2. Core principle: everything on your device

All operations — including reading documents, detecting personal data, masking and OCR — run only on this device. The content of your documents is never sent to any server, nor shared with us or third parties. Detection and masking work fully even in airplane mode. No network connection is used to process documents.

### 3. Data processed and where

The document text and images you provide to the App are processed only in your device's memory, for the duration of the detection and masking operation; once the operation finishes, the document text itself is not stored. In addition, limited technical/functional data may be kept on the device so the service can work: your app settings, language preference and (if enabled) history records. Subscription status and payment information are processed by Apple, NOT by us.

### 4. The only sensitive item stored on device

The label map generated during the AI workflow ([PERSON_1] → real value) is kept in the device's secure area (Keychain) so you can later decode the AI response. It stays only on this device, is not backed up to iCloud, and is never sent out. It is permanently deleted via Settings or the Clear action on the home screen. The masked outputs of your analyses and decoded AI responses are stored automatically for history — encrypted and only on this device; you can delete history records at any time from the History screen.

### 5. When does data leave the app?

Your document content leaves the App only when you copy and paste it somewhere — and even then, only in its masked form. The label legend (the real values behind the labels) is never included in the copied content and is never sent to the AI. You decide entirely what to share and where.

### 6. AI workflow

The App does not send any data to an AI service on its own. When you use the AI workflow, it only copies the masked text to an AI service of your choice (e.g. a chat app); when you paste the response back, the App decodes it using the label map on your device. Any third-party AI service you use is subject to its own privacy policy. AI outputs may be incorrect or fabricated (hallucinated).

### 7. Subscription and purchase data

Curtain Pro purchases are made through the Apple App Store (StoreKit); we do not see, collect or store your card and payment details — these are processed by Apple. RevenueCat, a subscription infrastructure service, is used to verify and manage subscription status. This verification covers only minimal data such as an anonymous user/device identifier and the purchase receipt, and NEVER includes your document content.

### 8. No analytics, tracking or ads

The App contains no ad networks, behavioral trackers or third-party analytics libraries. No identifying usage data is collected, no profiling is done, and your data is never sold or rented for advertising.

### 9. Third-party services

The App uses limited third-party services only for purchase and subscription functionality: Apple (App Store) for purchases and subscription management; RevenueCat for verifying subscription status. These providers process data only to deliver the relevant service, under their own privacy policies. Your document content is not transmitted to any of these services.

### 10. International transfers

Some of the service providers used for purchase and subscription verification (Apple, RevenueCat) may have servers located abroad; the limited subscription data in this scope may be processed abroad under the conditions required by the KVKK. Because your document data never leaves your device, it is not transferred abroad.

### 11. Data retention

Document text is not kept in memory once processing ends. The label map and history records you keep on the device remain only on your device until you delete them; you can permanently delete them at any time from Settings. When you remove the App from your device, this locally stored data is deleted as well.

### 12. Data security

The sensitive label map is stored in the device's Keychain, and history records are stored encrypted. You can protect access to the App with the optional App Lock (Face ID / Touch ID). Although industry-standard technical measures are applied, no system is 100% secure; you are responsible for your device security (screen lock, etc.).

### 13. Permissions

The App only requests permissions for the features you use: camera or photo access for document scanning/OCR. The permissions you grant are used only for the relevant feature, on the device; content obtained through this access is not sent out. You can revoke permissions at any time from your device settings.

### 14. Children's privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from this age group. If a parent/guardian believes their child has provided data through the App, they can contact us at the address below.

### 15. Your rights under the KVKK

Under Article 11 of the KVKK, you have the right to learn whether your personal data is being processed, request information, learn the purpose of processing, request that it be corrected if incomplete/incorrect, deleted or destroyed, and claim compensation if you suffer damages. Since most of your data in Curtain is kept only on your device, you can exercise most of these rights directly within the app (Clear / Delete history).

### 16. Limitations and disclaimer

Automatic detection may contain errors or omissions; an overlooked identifier may remain exposed, and even when names are masked the narrative may make a person identifiable. This tool alone does not guarantee KVKK compliance or legal anonymization. All outputs should be reviewed by a lawyer when necessary.

### 17. Transparency

Curtain's detection and masking run entirely and transparently on your device; no document data is ever sent out. The fonts used (Inter, JetBrains Mono — SIL Open Font License) are embedded in the app and never loaded from the internet.

### 18. Updates to this Policy

This Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version is always accessible within the app; continuing to use the App after significant changes means you accept the updated Policy.

### 19. Contact

For all questions and requests regarding privacy, you can reach us at curtainsupport@gmail.com.
